Hamburger

Homepage

This is a simple dinner option with very few ingredients and a quick cleanup.

Ingredients
  • Frozen hamburger patties
  • Hamburger buns
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Sauces
Steps
  1. Put frozen patty on cold pan and set heat to medium
  2. Flip patty over when the top no longer looks frozen (~ 5 minutes)
  3. Cook other side for about the same amount of time
  4. Take patty off pan and add cheese to melt
  5. Toast buns in the pan
  6. Serve up with your favorite condiments!
