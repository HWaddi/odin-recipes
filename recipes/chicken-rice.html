Chicken and Rice

Homepage

This is a quick and simple recipe for easy lunches. It only takes three ingredients and has a very simple cleanup.

Ingredients
  • 5 oz. canned chicken
  • Bag of Uncle Ben's microwave rice
  • Taco seasoning
Steps
  1. Preheat pan to medium heat
  2. Follow instructions to microwave rice
  3. Butter pan and heat up chicken for about a minute
  4. Add taco seasoning to taste and a splash of water
  5. Let chicken simmer for a couple minutes
  6. Serve/packup for lunch!
